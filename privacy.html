Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Who We Are

ShouldIMoveApp.com ("we," "us," or "our") is a cost-of-living and salary comparison tool for U.S. cities. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using ShouldIMoveApp.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

Information We Collect

Usage and Analytics Data. We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized information about how visitors interact with the site. This includes:

  • Pages visited and features used (e.g., which cities and jobs you compare)
  • Approximate geographic location (city/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Session duration and navigation patterns
  • Referring website (how you found us)

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

User Inputs. The city, job, and salary selections you make in the comparison tool are processed in your browser. We do not store individual comparison inputs on our servers at this time; however, aggregate patterns derived from analytics (e.g., which city pairs are most frequently compared) are captured through Google Analytics.

Account and Payment Data (Pro subscribers). If you subscribe to ShouldIMoveApp Pro, we collect your email address and payment information. Payment processing is handled by a third-party processor; we do not store full credit card numbers. We retain your email address for account management, product updates, and marketing communications related to our services.

Communications. If you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence to respond to your inquiry and to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve ShouldIMoveApp.com
  • To understand how the tool is used and which features are most valuable
  • To send transactional emails (e.g., subscription confirmations, receipts)
  • To send product updates and promotional communications to Pro subscribers (you may opt out at any time)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with applicable laws
  • To develop, analyze, and distribute aggregated or de-identified datasets and insights derived from usage patterns (see "Data Sharing and Sale" below)

Data Sharing and Sale

We may share or sell data in the following circumstances:

Aggregated and De-identified Data. We may share, license, or sell aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified data about usage patterns — such as which cities are most frequently compared, which professions users research, or regional relocation trends — with third parties including research organizations, real estate companies, employers, and data analytics firms. This data does not identify individual users.

Service Providers. We share data with trusted vendors who help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing). These vendors are contractually restricted from using your data for their own purposes.

Business Transfers. If ShouldIMoveApp.com is acquired, merged, or its assets are sold, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

Legal Requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.

We do not sell personal information such as your name, email address, or payment information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some functionality. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Your Rights

California Residents (CCPA). If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, or sell
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact.shouldimoveapp@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days.

All Users. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by contacting us at the address above. Note that we may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Data Retention

We retain analytics data as permitted by Google Analytics' default retention settings (up to 14 months). Email addresses for Pro subscribers are retained for the duration of your subscription and for a reasonable period thereafter. We may retain de-identified and aggregated data indefinitely.

Children's Privacy

ShouldIMoveApp.com is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
