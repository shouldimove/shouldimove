Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the salary and cost-of-living data come from?

Salary estimates are based on Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics (OEWS) data, adjusted for local cost of living. Cost-of-living indices are derived from publicly available sources. All figures are estimates intended for general planning — they are not a substitute for employer-specific research.

How often is the data updated?

We aim to update salary data annually when the BLS releases new OEWS figures, typically each spring. Cost-of-living data is refreshed on a similar schedule.

Can I request that a city or profession be added?

Yes — email us with your suggestion and we'll consider it for the next update. Cities and professions with the highest relocation search volume are prioritized.

What is ShouldIMoveApp Pro?

Pro is a paid subscription that unlocks advanced comparison features, detailed breakdowns, and multi-city analysis. See the main app for current pricing and features.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Email us at contact.shouldimoveapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Cancel Subscription" and we'll process your cancellation promptly.

How do I opt out of data collection or request deletion of my data?

See our Privacy Policy for full details. To submit a request, email us at contact.shouldimoveapp@gmail.com with the subject "Privacy Request."
